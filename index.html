Gina & Faisal

July 7, 2018

stories
Her Story

Faisal and I met one cold evening over a cup of Starbucks Coffee. He'll probably tell you otherwise, but Starbucks brought us together.

- Gina

His Story

Gina and I met one summer morning at the sandy shores of Chicago. I was flying a kite, as was she, and then our kites intertwined. She'll probably tell you otherwise, but two kites brought us together.

- Faisal

Our Story

So the truth is, we met through friends. And between the both of us, I may be a better storyteller, because there were no kites involved in us meeting. In fact, she has never flown a kite in her life. But our first date did take place at Starbucks. We became fast friends and I quickly realized that if you like it, put a ring on it - so I did.

- Faisal
